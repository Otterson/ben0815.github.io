Rush













Theta Tau has two rounds of rush, one at the beginning of each semester. For prospective members we run booths at both the MSC open house and at E-week by the Zachary Engineering building. We also host tailgates before home football games and will have groups set up around campus to help students find out more about the organization. During the rush process we host social events to help rushees meet active members and get a feel for the organization. Students are encouraged to attend as many events as possible during rush. At the end of the process, active members will choose who will receive invitations to continue on as pledges.

There is NO obligation to pledge just because you attend rush events. Come by and say Hi!
